Why a retreat?

We are all gathering in Xiamen for a small week to work together in better environment. These are not holidays, see it as a more relaxed company time in a different environment.

The main goal of the retreat is to get to know the team. Make sure you do not always stay with your usual collegues and mingle. Do not be afraid, none of them bite!

We will also have activites, some optional, some mandatory. Come and join as many as you want and let's have a blast!

Make sure to let the Operations team know if you need anything. We are not here to nurse you, but we can try to help!

FAQ

A few things that are important for you to make the stay of the team as awesome as it can.

What costs are covered by the team?

  • Trains
  • Buses (train to hotel, hotel to harbor, hotel to downtown)
  • Boat round trip to Gulangyu
  • Hotel & Breakfast (Nov 22 - Nov 27, Dec 6 - Dec 11 & Dec 13 - Dec 18)
  • Team dinner (Nov 24, Dec 8 & Dec 15)
  • Team dinner (Nov 25, Dec 9 & Dec 16)
Everything else is not covered.

Do I need to bring my laptop during the retreat?

Yes, mornings are working period.

Can I adjust my working schedule differently?

It depends, make sure your team is aware, that the customer is aware, and that it doesn't prevent you from attending the team building activities. You're expected to still work 4h per day.

Where will we work during the retreat?

It is up to each team leader to decide where the team will work during the retreat.

What about COVID, PCR tests and QR codes?

You will need a 48h PCR test to board the train. Be sure to have been tested earlier.

We included 2 tests on the first day - one in Shanghai before the departure, one in Xiamen when just arrived. We may have to wait for the results from Xiamen's test before being provided access to our rooms.

We encourage the team to proceed to regular PCR tests (every 48h), the closest testing booth is available next to the KFC (5min by walk from the hotel).

Upon return in Shanghai, proceed to daily tests for 3 days to ensure your QR code doesn't turn to yellow!

Can I stay longer ?

You can, but at your own cost. If you plan to do so, let us know in advance, especially if you want to enjoy our rate at the resort...

Follow the regular process for holiday / leaves, and ensure you notify your leader / HR of your holidays ASAP.
